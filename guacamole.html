Guacamole Recipe

This is the second paragraph describing the recipe. Make sure you follow it step-by-step otherwise it wont be as good! I also recommend dipping oreos in it! If you want this excellent guac, here's the list of ingredients needed!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 3 large red onions
  • 4 cups salt
  • 2 cups minced garlic
  • 2 cups black pepper
  • 1 cup cayanne pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cups lime juice

Steps

  1. Cut open the avocados, remove the core and place avocado pulp in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Dice onions and place into the large mixing bowl.
  3. Pour in lime juice.
  4. Pour in minced garlic.
  5. Add in all remaining spices.
  6. Mix well (preferably with a large fork) until all ingredients are combined.
  7. Now enjoy with oreo cookies!
